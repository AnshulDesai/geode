# Design Context

## Users
Content creators, marketers, and developers who care about AI search visibility. They're evaluating their own content or a client's. They want clear, actionable feedback — not vanity metrics. They run geode locally because they value privacy (BYOK).

## Brand Personality
**Refined. Precise. Grounded.**

geode is a tool for people who care about craft. The name evokes geology — something natural, layered, beautiful when you look closely. The interface should feel like a well-designed instrument, not a dashboard full of charts.

## Aesthetic Direction
- **Artistic and editorial** — closer to a design portfolio than a SaaS dashboard
- **Dark mode primary** — deep, warm darks (not pure black), with accent colors that feel intentional
- **Typography-forward** — type hierarchy does the heavy lifting, not boxes and borders
- **Restrained color** — mostly monochrome with strategic color for scores and priority
- **Subtle texture** — not flat, not skeuomorphic. Depth through spacing and contrast, not shadows
- **Anti-references:** generic SaaS dashboards, gamified SEO tools, vibecoded AI slop with gradients and glassmorphism

## Typography
- **Headings:** Inter or system sans-serif, 600 weight, tight letter-spacing (-0.02em)
- **Body:** System sans-serif, 400 weight, 14-15px base
- **Scores/numbers:** Tabular numerals, 700 weight, slightly larger
- **Monospace accents:** For URLs, technical details — JetBrains Mono or system monospace

## Spacing
- Base unit: 4px
- Component padding: 12-16px
- Section gaps: 24-32px
- Max content width: 1200px (split panel)

## Design Principles

1. **Show, don't decorate.** Every visual element earns its place. No ornamental gradients, no decorative illustrations, no filler.
2. **Scores are the hero.** The overall score and category bars should be the first thing your eye lands on. Everything else supports them.
3. **Actionable over informational.** Action items are more important than findings. The UI should make "what to fix" more prominent than "what we found."
4. **Respect the content.** The page preview panel shows someone's work. The inspector overlay should be subtle (warm highlight, not aggressive red boxes).
5. **Craft signals craft.** geode evaluates content quality — the tool itself must demonstrate quality. Sloppy UI undermines the product's credibility.
